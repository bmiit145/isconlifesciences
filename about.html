Mr. Kamaljeet Jaiswal

Director Profile

Mr. Kamaljeet Jaiswal is the Promoter & Director of ISCON Life Sciences, one of the leading manufacturer, exporter, and supplier of health care and pharmaceutical products.

Mr. Jaiswal is a postgraduate in commerce and has an overall work experience of more than 24 years. He established ISCON Life Sciences in 2004 and since then has proved his acumen in the sector by establishing it as one of India’s leading pharmaceutical group of companies.

Mr. Jaiswal loves to be updated with the latest technological advances in the market and it has tremendously helped his company receive an edge over its competitors.

Mr. Jaiswal’s long term goal is to take his business to the next level and build an equity-based company. At present, the range of ISCON Life Sciences products includes antibiotics, anti-infectives, digestive enzymes, vitamin supplements, injections, cosmetics, EYE/ENT drops, and psychiatric drugs and there is constant research and development for newer and better products.

ISCON Life Sciences has built a reputation as a trusted brand not just in India but also in the international markets. The company has partnered with some of the top online directories such as Trade India and India Mart.

For its outstanding contribution in the fields of medicine and healthcare, ISCON Life Sciences was conferred with the Business Excellence Award in 2016. With their dedication towards serving the society with quality healthcare products, ISCON Life Sciences effectively led by its founder-director is set to scale new heights in the pharmaceutical industry.
Jaiswal is an avid traveler and likes singing and practicing silence to keep himself calm and face tough situations.
